Utilities for OpenStreetmap
These Utilities can be used to get the cordinates for OpenStreetmap that would match your Lidar/DEM area outline. Most likely the OPCD Inner

coordinates.py Simply edit this file with your coordinates form your Lidar/DEM properties and it will generate a report with the coordinates for matching OpenStreetMaps for export of you 'map.osm' scaled to match you elevation maps.

crs_transform.py takes a geotiff image (DEM) image and does the same report giving you corrdinates matching OpenStreetMaps.
